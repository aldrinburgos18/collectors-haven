FREE

Here at Collectors Haven, we love video games and have a huge passion for the industry. Like most gamers, we love to collect and play games, as well as constantly discover new games we've never heard of. We want to share that love and passion with the internet and the existing video game community. That's why we've decided, together as a team, to make our website and services 100% free. We think that games should be shared with any and everyone of all ages and backgrounds. Going free means we can give the ability to discover as many games as you want for as long as you want. Enjoy our creation and give us feedback to continue on making our platform the best we can make it. The better we can make it, the more people that will use it, and that means we can share the love to thousands and eventually millions.
